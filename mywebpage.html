Historic Places in Johannesburg

Constitution Hill

It housed political activists like Nelson Mandela, Gandhi, and Winnie Madikizela-Mandela. The Constitutional Court now stands on the site. It offers guided tours exploring its rich history.

Apartheid Museum

It features interactive exhibits, including personal accounts and artifacts. The museum's architecture symbolizes the apartheid regime's impact. It has separate entrances for different racial groups.

Cradle of Humankind

It's home to the Sterkfontein Caves, where the famous "Mrs. Ples" and "Little Foot" fossils were found. Visitors can explore the caves. Visitors can learn about human evolution through interactive exhibits.
